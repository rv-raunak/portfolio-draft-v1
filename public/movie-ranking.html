My Favourite Movies of All Time - By RV Raunak

Top 3 movie recommmendations for you

Taqdeer(2017)

A movie close to my heart. One of the best full stack movies of all time with romance as a forte. It has one of my favoruite tunes of all time.

Your Name

My first anime movie ever and also maybe still the best anime movie I have watched till now. It connects to be both spiritually and deeply.

Hit 3

One of the most brutal and entertaining action-thriller films of all time. It is the latest installment of the hit series as of now. And brings back some nostalgic characters together.
